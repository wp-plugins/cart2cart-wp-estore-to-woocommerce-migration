== Description ==
Cart2Cart: WordPress eStore Plugin to WooCommerce Migration plugin is an effective and intuitive tool that allows to perform data transfer from one platform to another effortlessly.  Due to its automated nature, it doesn’t require any programming expertise to or specific knowledge. 
The algorithm of your actions is simple: establish a connection between the stores, choose the entities you want to transfer and adjust the migration to your needs with the help of additional options. Afterwards, you’ll be offered to proceed with free Demo Migration to test how plugin works by transferring up to 10 entities to WooCommerce. Either way, you can just launch Full Migration at once and let Cart2Cart handle everything else. Note, the plugin itself is free to download, but migration is paid and starts from $69.

= Features =
* **Free Demo Migration.** Take a look at how the plugin works by performing a costless Demo Migration before conducting an actual one. 
* **Flexible Price.** Pay only for what you get - migration price fully depends on the amount of transferring entities and additional options chosen. 
* **Qualified Support.** Contact Cart2Cart professional Support Team and get the assistance you require. 
* **No Downtime.** Cart2Cart Migration plugin has no negative impact on the work of your current store - it will be fully available for customers during the whole migration process.

**Transfer the following data from WordPress eStore Plugin to WooCommerce:**

* Products, product images, product variants;
* Categories, category images;
* Customers, customer shipping address, customer billing address;
* Orders, order statuses, order products;
* Taxes.

*Supported WooCommerce versions:* 1.1.x - 1.6.x, 2.x
**Please note:** Cart2Cart moves data only. It means your current store design won’t be migrated. 

In order to perform an accurate transfer from WordPress eStore Plugin to WooCommerce, you need to follow a few simple steps. To begin with, you have to download the migration plugin, install it and establish the connection between your stores. Afterwards, you’ll be automatically redirected to Cart2Cart website in order to continue migration. To test the plugin’s functionality, proceed with free Demo Migration (optionally). Finally, select Insurance plan (not necessary, but recommended to save your money in case of remigration) and launch Full Migration to start automated data transfer. 

**Pre-Migration Steps**
1. Install WooCommerce store and ensure its accessible via internet.
1. Check for FTP details for your shopping carts. You need them to perform migration.

== Installation ==
1. Download plugin.zip file and extract it to your hard drive
1. Upload unzipped file to wp-content/plugin directory
1. Activate “Cart2Cart WordPress eStore Plugin to WooCommerce Migration” in plugins section of your admin panel
1. Insert FTP details to upload the Connection Bridge files for WordPress eStore Plugin
1. Connection Bridge files for WooCommerce are will be uploaded automatically
1. Click Start Migration button and follow the Migration Wizard to perform an automated migration

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do you migrate customer’s credit cards information? =
Currently, Cart2Cart doesn’t support migrations of credit card’s data, however customer’s billing and shipping addresses will be transferred automatically.

= Will you transfer my SEO URLs? =
No, migrations of SEO URLs from WordPress eStore Plugin to WooCommerce are not available. Also, note that your store’s URLs and structure will be changed after the transfer.

= Will my current WordPress eStore Plugin function during the migration? =
Migration process won't affect your source shopping cart in any way. It will be up and running successfully during and after the migration process.
